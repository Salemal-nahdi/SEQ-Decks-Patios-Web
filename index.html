About SEQ Decks & Patios

Welcome to SEQ Decks & Patios, your trusted partner for creating beautiful and functional outdoor living spaces across South East Queensland.

With years of experience in the industry, we've built a reputation for quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and exceptional customer service.

We specialise in designing and building custom decks, patios, pergolas, and outdoor entertainment areas that enhance your home's value and provide the perfect space for relaxation and enjoyment.

Our Values

Quality craftsmanship in every project

Our Services

Custom Decks

Transform your outdoor space with a beautiful custom deck designed to suit your home's style and your family's needs. We specialise in timber decks that add value to your property while creating the perfect outdoor entertaining area.

  • Premium treated timber and composite materials
  • Custom designs for any space or terrain
  • Professional installation with attention to detail
  • Specialising in timber and composite decks

Patios & Pergolas

Create a comfortable outdoor living area that's protected from the elements with our custom-built patios and pergolas. Perfect for entertaining, outdoor dining, or simply enjoying your garden in comfort regardless of the weather.

  • Insulated and non-insulated roofing options
  • Custom designs to match your home's architecture
  • High-quality materials for durability in Queensland's climate

Outdoor Entertainment Areas

Create the ultimate outdoor living space for entertaining family and friends with our custom-designed entertainment areas. From simple BBQ areas to complete outdoor kitchens and living rooms, we can help you make the most of Queensland's outdoor lifestyle.

  • Custom outdoor kitchens and BBQ areas
  • Comfortable seating and dining spaces
  • Weather-resistant materials for durability
